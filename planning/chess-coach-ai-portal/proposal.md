## Why

Chess coaches currently lack a unified, intelligent system to track student progress, personalize coaching, and communicate with parents — leading to fragmented insights, missed growth opportunities, and inefficient session planning. This portal addresses that gap by giving coaches an AI-powered command center and students an intelligent mentor, all in one platform.

## What Changes

- Introduce an AI Assistant Portal purpose-built for chess coaching
- Students get a personalized AI mentor that tracks their history, weaknesses, and goals
- Coaches get a real-time dashboard with per-student progress analytics and AI-generated coaching suggestions
- Parents receive automated, structured status reports on their child's progress
- Target-based learning plans are generated and tracked per student per month
- Health and mental coaching tips are integrated alongside chess improvement plans
- All context (student history, past games, improvement areas) is persisted and used to personalize every interaction

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `student-profile`: Stores and manages personal context for each student — history, skill level, strengths, weaknesses, goals
- `game-analysis`: AI-powered analysis of student games with move-by-move feedback and pattern detection
- `progress-tracking`: Monthly and overall progress metrics per student — rating trend, topic mastery, win/loss patterns
- `improvement-plan`: Target-based learning roadmap with topics, milestones, and deadlines per student
- `ai-mentor`: Student-facing AI mentor that answers questions, explains concepts, and encourages based on personal context
- `coach-dashboard`: Coach-facing view of all students with status summaries, alerts, and AI coaching suggestions
- `health-coach`: Wellness and mental performance tips integrated into student sessions and plans
- `parent-report`: Automated status report generation and delivery to parents with progress highlights and next steps
- `communication-hub`: Centralized messaging and report delivery between coach, student, and parents
